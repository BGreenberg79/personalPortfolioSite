About Me

I am a former NFL Scout and Cornell University graduate who decided to challenge himself by pivoting to a career in Software Development. I graduated from The Nolan School of Hotel Administration in 2016 with a Bachelors of Science degree, and upon graduation spent a year with the Cleveland Browns scouting department. I later utilized my degree as a Walt Disney World Cast Member making magic for guests staying at the Wilderness Lodge Hotel. After my time with the Walt Disney Company ended, I moved back to New York where I started coaching high school football and working with a sports and education focused non-profit organization, About U Outreach. During this time I also worked as a Business Development Representative providing sales support for SaaS start ups, while working for Victory Lap. All of these experiences led me to realize that the most valuable tool a professional can have is technical knowledge and sparked my curiosity in programming. With this new found direction I decided to enroll in Coding Temple's Software Development program where I completed my study of Python, HTML/CSS, React, and SQL. Most recently, I have formed a trading card and collectible focused business Rip & Raw Trading Card LLC which will combine my passion for sports with my knowledge of technology and e-commerce. I also am currently pursuing roles at tech organizations to continue my path as a lifelong learner by furthering my programming skills as a Software Engineer or Product Manager.
